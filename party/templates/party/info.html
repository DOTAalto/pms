Telegram

You should definitely join the Telegram chat of the event. To avoid bot spam, we don't post the direct link here, but you can find the channel by searching @graffathon in Telegram.

The chat is the main communication channel during the event and is also the quickest way to contact the organizers. Don't hesitate to also ask for help with programming or anything else!

Safer space policy

At Graffathon, we follow the safer space policy of the Aalto University Student Union (AYY)

If you experience or witness any form of harassment or discrimination, please talk to our harassment contacts (@felixbade, @fionakeil, @jonnakonna) or any organizer you feel comfortable with
